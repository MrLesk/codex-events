If these Vienna Winner Terms conflict with the platform terms for this hackathon, these Vienna Winner Terms control to the extent of the conflict.

## 1. Relationship to the Vienna application terms

- The Vienna application terms and other published Vienna event rules continue to apply to selected winners.

- These Vienna Winner Terms govern prize acceptance, redemption, and fulfilment for this hackathon.

## 2. Scope of awards

- The current Vienna prize configuration published in Codex Hackathons includes team-scoped OpenAI API credit awards for ranks 1 through 3 and a member-scoped `1 year ChatGPT Pro` benefit for members of the top five teams.
- Prize descriptions, rank ranges, and award scopes are the ones published for this hackathon in Codex Hackathons at the time winners are announced.

## 3. Team-scoped and member-scoped redemption

- A team-scoped prize is redeemed through Codex Hackathons by an active team admin acting for the winning team.
- A member-scoped prize is redeemed individually by the eligible recipient identified through the platform's prize-eligibility records.
- For member-scoped Vienna prizes, the eligible recipients are the prize-eligible team members frozen for this hackathon when prize eligibility was locked.
- The platform records the exact Vienna winner-terms version accepted for each redemption.

- OpenAI API credit prizes for ranks 1 through 3 remain subject to OpenAI's then-current credit, account, and service rules, including any non-transferability, expiry, or workspace restrictions imposed by the provider.
